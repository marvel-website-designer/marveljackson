Discovery Call

We kick things off with a discovery call where I listen closely to your goals, business vision, and specific needs for the website. This is where I gather all the essential details, answer your questions, discuss possible features, functionality, and design preferences, and ensure we are aligned with your vision.

Project Estimate

Once I understand your requirements, I provide a project estimate that outlines the cost, timeline, and deliverables for the website. This detailed estimate ensures that there are no flaws down the line and that we are working within the set budget and expectations from the very beginning.

Website Security & Backups

I prioritize your website’s security with robust protection, regular updates, and threat prevention. Automated backups ensure your data is safe and easily restorable in case of hacks, errors, or failures—minimizing downtime and protecting your online presence.

Responsive Design Phase

Here is where I focus on responsive design, ensuring that your website looks fantastic and optimized on all devices—whether it’s a desktop, tablet, or smartphone. This phase ensures that your visitors have a seamless and enjoyable experience across all viewpoint.
